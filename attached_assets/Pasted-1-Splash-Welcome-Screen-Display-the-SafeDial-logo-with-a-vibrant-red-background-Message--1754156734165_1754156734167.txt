🎨 UI/UX Theme Requirements

Apply these design rules for a clean, modern, and responsive look:

🎨 Color Palette
	•	Primary: Red #D32F2F
	•	Accent: Orange #FF6F00 or Blue #1976D2
	•	Background: Light gradient (white to blush red)
	•	Text: Dark Gray #212121

✍️ Typography
	•	Use Google Fonts: Roboto, Poppins, or Montserrat
	•	Headings: bold, large size
	•	Body text: medium size, high contrast

🧩 Buttons & Inputs
	•	Use Material Design 3 or Jetpack Compose’s MaterialTheme
	•	Rounded corners (16dp+)
	•	Icons from Material Icons
	•	Ripple effects and smooth transitions

🌙 Dark Mode Support
	•	Background: #121212
	•	Text: #FFFFFF
	•	Emergency buttons retain bright red

🗺️ Map Styling
	•	Custom styled map (light/dark)
	•	Emergency pins with icons (police, hospital, fire, etc.)

✨ Animations
	•	Fade/slide screen transitions
	•	Button click animations
	•	Pulse/glow for SOS buttons
